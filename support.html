Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a package?

To buy a package, navigate to the Buy Package page from the sidebar. Select your preferred plan (6 Months, 12 Months, or 18 Months), enter the amount, choose your wallet, and click Buy Now. The RND tokens will be locked in your account and daily releases will start from the next day.

How does the referral commission work?

Our referral system offers 5 levels of commission:

  • Level 1: 8% (Direct Referrals)
  • Level 2: 4%
  • Level 3: 2%
  • Level 4: 1%
  • Level 5: 1%

When your referred friends purchase packages, you earn commission on their investments. The commission is automatically credited to your referral wallet.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment depends on the plan:

  • 6 Months Plan: $10 USDT
  • 12 Months Plan: $100 USDT
  • 18 Months Plan: $200 USDT
How do I withdraw my earnings?

To withdraw your funds:

  1. Go to the Withdraw page from the sidebar.
  2. Select your wallet (Deposit, Referral, or RND Wallet).
  3. Enter the amount (minimum 10 USDT/RND).
  4. Enter your external wallet address (BEP-20).
  5. Click Withdraw and wait for processing (24-48 hours).
What happens to my daily releases?

Your daily releases are automatically calculated and credited to your RND wallet every day. If you miss a day, the releases are accumulated and credited when you log in next time. The release amount depends on your package and the remaining RND in your locked wallet.

Is there any fee for deposits or withdrawals?

Deposits: 0% fee.

Withdrawals: 1 USDT fee for USDT withdrawals (BEP-20). RND withdrawals have 0 fee.
